The Standard

Bodywork built on sport science

The Athletic Standard specializes in sports massage and athletic rehabilitation. We have been in the sports world since 2014 and provide personalized, educated massage therapy, no matter your athletic background.

From tour-level competitors to weekend runners, every session starts with how you actually move — and ends with a plan to keep you moving.

  • Personalized

    Sessions built around your sport, your training load, and what your body needs right now.

  • Educated

    Licensed massage therapists with kinesiology and exercise-science backgrounds.

  • For every athlete

    Pros, amateurs, and everyday movers — recovery and rehabilitation for all levels.

The therapists

Who's on the table side

  • Mike Martin

    LMT, BSKin, MMP

    Mike has been in the professional fitness world since 2014, when a college internship turned into a career. Originally headed for physical therapy, he switched to kinesiology with a focus on sport and exercise science. Six years, three gyms, and a lot of learning as a personal trainer led him to sports massage and athletic rehabilitation.

  • Brian Gscheidle

    LMT, MsK, CPT, MMP

    Brian has dedicated his life to serving the community and promoting healthy patterns of movement. His passion began with his own weight-loss journey in high school — over 50 pounds in a single summer. After undergrad and grad school in kinesiology at Augusta University, he pursued massage therapy as a more direct way to help people return to their sports and lives after injury.

  • Brennan Kirby

    LMT

    Brennan graduated in 2013 from NC Massage School in North Carolina. Her modalities are Swedish, deep tissue, and sports recovery and maintenance. When she's not in the studio, she's out on distance runs or spending time with her husband and two kids.
